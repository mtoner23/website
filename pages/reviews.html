Tlahco

1 Star

Lincoln Park's newest taco joint had a lot of promise. Instagrammable tortilla colors. Hand drawn chalk menu. Goregous new Interior. Killer location. They boast of their authenticity and how they are family owned. All great qualities in todays modern world. The issues start with the tacos.

BlueBird

5 Stars

God I love bluebird. Big sandwiches, great prices, fantastic sides, and wonderful sauces. Bluebird hits it out of the park again and again. Try the German Bird, or the Bluebird and get any of the sides; none of them will let you down. This is the spot I bring people to, to sell them on Chicago. Its around the corner from me and better than any brew pub sandwich you can get in the suburbs, for the same price. Next time you're in lakeview you have to stop in bluebird.
